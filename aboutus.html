    African Tech Community aims to create awareness and share opportunities for young people who are enthusiast in digital tech skills and want to learn, gain mastery and get remote jobs.

    1

    What We've Done

    - Established Africa Tech Community as a reputable organization promotingtechnology and digital literacy

    - Developed a network of tech enthusiasts and industry experts

    - Help members to get remote jobs and acquired relevant digital skills.

    - Successfully organized tech hangouts and weekly webinar training programs

    2

    What We Plan To Do

    - Create Positive Awareness through tech advocacy

    - Establish a tech club Across different Public universities in Nigeria and beyond.

    - offer learning support to students.

    3

    What We Plan To Do

    - Breaking down stereotype and changing the narrative about African talents, showcasing their exceptional skills and contributions to the global tech industry.

    - Providing access to internet-related opportunities, training, and resources for students and graduates to transition into tech careers and remote work, bridging the gap to employment.

    - Ensuring that every member of the Africa Tech Community earns a substantial income, fostering financial stability and growth, and promoting a brighter future for African talents.

    4

    Our Goals

    - Educate students about the vast possibilities in the tech industry

    - Enhance skills and employability for students/Unemployed graduates.

    - Increase innovation and entrepreneurship in Africa.

    5

    Our Mission

    - Breaking down stereotype and changing the narrative about African talents, showcasing their exceptional skills and contributions to the global tech industry.

    - Providing access to internet-related opportunities, training, and resources for students and graduates to transition into tech careers and remote work, bridging the gap to employment.

    - Ensuring that every member of the Africa Tech Community earns a substantial income, fostering financial stability and growth, and promoting a brighter future for African talents.
